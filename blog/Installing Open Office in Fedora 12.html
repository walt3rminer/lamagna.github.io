Installing Open Office in Fedora 12

Here is the simple step that describes how to install Open Office in Fedora 12. 
yum -y install openoffice.org-core \
openoffice.org-calc \
openoffice.org-base \
openoffice.org-draw \
openoffice.org-impress \
openoffice.org-langpack-es \
openoffice.org-writer \
hunspell-es \
openoffice.org-extendedPDF
