Well, we are looking for a fast solution here. You can do this:
vi /etc/apt/sources.list

%s/us\.archive\.ubuntu/old-releases.ubuntu/g
%s/us\.security\.ubuntu/old-releases.ubuntu/g
These are commands in the vi text editor. You may have to customize the command, if you are in Argentina it may be ar.archive and ar.security, so the first section of the regular expression should be that. In case of doubt, bring here your question, it is always welcome.
