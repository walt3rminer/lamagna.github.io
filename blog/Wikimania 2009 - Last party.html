Wikimania 2009 - Last party

The end of Wiki09 - Buenos Aires. The 3rd day of this international congress has always a party, and in this case it took place in Down Town, Buenos Aires. The party was in a nice "Boliche", started a 22:00 and when we arrived there was a lot of people already, it is not a coincidence that the meeting had an incredible high number of german participants.

I was enchanted because i asked if i could go with my wife and on the Wiki09 they gave me a ticket for one peron more than me, this is worth to tell because i had to ask to know it, since it was not written anywere.

The party was relaxing and it caused me a nice sensation while looking at all the people that participated from the Wiki09 in a different context. It is impossible to translate with words this emotion but i have to tell it, to invite anybody that may have the opportunity to go to Wiki10 for example (which is taking place in Poland) to participate.

On the meeting it was plenty of food, free drinks, ladies and music. It was a private event, but you could take a friend to the party.

It was a special moment for me, those special moments you keep forever , by beeing with my wife in the party, with the peopele i have talked about evey day of these 4 days of event... and about the people that not only is on the media but also is influent, entrepreneur and may look different on the distance...Wikimedia Board members.
