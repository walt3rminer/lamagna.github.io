Resolve Cannot retrieve metalink for repository

This happens when yum is unable to access its repositories.  In Fedora 11 it can be resolved this was but in Fedora 12 it happened to me that in my office i use a proxy to connect to Internet but not at home.  So, you may have to set up a proxy in /etc/yum.conf or not.

To set a proxy modify /etc/yum.conf and add:
proxy=http://the.proxy.address:8080/

This should be enough but you can also specify a user and password.

I hope it helped.
