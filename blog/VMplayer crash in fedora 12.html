VMplayer crash in fedora 12

After installing VMplayer from the bundle, the 3.0.1 version where i got surprised because it crashed just one second after running it.  Thanks that another co-blogger posted the solution, too simple in fact:


cd /usr/lib/vmware/lib
mv libcurl.so.4 libcurl.so.4.old
If you need to recompile the modules because some of them is failing, you can do it this way: 
vmware-modconfig --console --install-all
