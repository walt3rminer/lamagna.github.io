This is a Live Firewall CD i called "Red Firewall", i am the author of it.
Special thanks to my friends from the Perl Mongers Group (http://cafe.pm.org) for listening and inspiring me to add more features.

The idea behind it is that you can have a gateway/firewall to Internet
without a hard disk:
Advantages:
  • Hacking proof - while it is a live usb/cd it manages a readonly medium and it is just a mather of rebooting
  • If there is a powercut, no problem, the hard disk would have no filesystem issues
  • Less costs of hard disk management
It is in BETA state, please download it and try it with your network interfaces, if it do not recognizes them let me know.

Be patient, do not think bad about me because it do not achieves your expectations,
it is still under development in my spare time.
